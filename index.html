Building reliable learning systems for audio, multimodal intelligence, and scientific discovery.

I am an Associate Professor at the National University of Defense Technology, working on computer audition, multimodal continual learning, and AI4Science. My group studies practical machine learning systems that remain useful under data shift, limited labels, and complex scientific constraints.

Research Focus

Questions I Care About

01

Computer Audition

General audio signal processing, robust sound understanding, and learning methods for acoustic scenes with complex noise and limited annotations.

02

Continual Learning

Models that absorb new data and tasks without forgetting earlier knowledge, with emphasis on reliable deployment in changing environments.

03

AI4Science

Data-driven materials science, active learning, and machine learning pipelines that accelerate discovery while respecting scientific evidence.
